Cheesy Chicken and Broccoli bake

How to make a cheesy chicken and broccoli bake

Ingredients

  • 450 g Blanched broccoli, chopped
  • 400 g Cooked chicken fillets, chopped
  • 75 g Butter
  • 480 ml Milk
  • 300 g Cheddar cheese
  • 480 ml Chicken stock
  • 40 g Cornflour
  • 120 ml water
  • Salt and pepper

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C
  2. Grease the baking dish with butter
  3. Layer the dish with alternating layers of broccoli and chicken
  4. Dissolve the cornflour in the water
  5. Heat a saucepan up on the stove to a medium heat
  6. Melt the butter in the saucepan
  7. Add the cornflour mix, the chicken stock, and milk to the saucepan
  8. Add salt, pepper, and seasoning to taste
  9. Stir the sauce until it thickens
  10. Reduce the heat to low
  11. Stir in half of the cheddar cheese to melt
  12. Pour the sauce over the chicken and broccoli
  13. Top with the remainder of the cheddar cheese
  14. Bake in the over for 30 minutes, or until golden brown and bubbling
