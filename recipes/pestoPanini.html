Chicken Pesto Panini

How to make a chicken pesto panini

Ingredients

  • □ 1 Baguette
  • □ 1 tbsp Basil pesto
  • □ 4 Sun-dried tomatoes
  • □ 25 g Cheese
  • □ 175 g Roast chicken, sliced
  • □ 1 Handful baby spinach leaves
  • □ Olive oil
  • □ Salt and pepper

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the snackwicher
  2. Slice the baguette in half and pull out some of the insides from each half
  3. Brush the outsides of the bread with olive oil
  4. Spread the pesto on the inside of the baguette
  5. Top with spinach, sun-dried tomatoes, chicken, and cheese
  6. Season with salt and pepper
  7. Press closed and toast
