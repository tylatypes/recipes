BLT Pizza

How to make a BLT Pizza

Ingredients

  • 300 g Pizza base
  • 2 Sliced tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp Dried oregano
  • 2 tsp Dried basil
  • Bacon pieces
  • 120 g Mozzarella
  • 120 g Mayo
  • Garlic

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C
  2. Roll out the dough and crimp the edges
  3. Arrange the tomato slices over the dough
  4. Sprinkle with the oregano and half of the basil
  5. Top with bacon and cheese
  6. Bake for 15 - 20 minutes, or until golden brown
  7. Whisk together the mayo, garlic, and remaining garlic to drizzle over the pizza
