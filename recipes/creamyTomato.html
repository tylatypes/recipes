Creamy tomato & sausage pasta

How to make creamy tomato and sausage pasta

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tbsp Olive oil
  • Sliced sausages
  • 1/2 an Onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Dried herbs
  • 400 g tomato passata
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 50 ml cream
  • 60 g Parmesan cheese
  • Bacon rashes

Recipe:

  1. Cook the sausage in a frying pan with the olive oil on medium-high heat for 5 minutes, or until the sausages have browned
  2. Remove the sausage from the pan and reduce the heat to medium-low
  3. Add the bacon rashes and onions to the frying pan and cook until the rashes are crisp and the onions are soft
  4. Mix in the garlic and herbs until the you can smell the rich fragrance (about 1 minute)
  5. Add the sausage back to the frying pan and stir in the tomato passata and puree
  6. Increase the heat and bring the sauce to a simmer. Allow to cook for about 10 minutes, or until thickened
  7. Add the Parmesan cheese and cream to the sauce
  8. Make the pasta
  9. Combine and enjoy!
