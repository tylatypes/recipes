Chicken wrap

How to make a chicken wrap

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Sweetcorn
  • 1 Tortilla wrap
  • 70 g Chicken
  • 1/2 Avo
  • 1 tbsp Salsa
  • 3 tbsp Cheddar cheese, grated
  • 1 Handful of lettuce

Recipe:

  1. Heat the sweetcorn and salsa until warm
  2. Spread the sweetcorn and salsa onto the tortilla
  3. Top with chicken, lettuce, avo, and cheddar cheese
  4. Roll up tightly
